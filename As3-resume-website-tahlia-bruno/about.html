Tahlia's Story

Tahlia is a passionate creator and booklover, She worked as a barista bar and gaming attendant within the Western Sydney Region, has travelled overseas to Japan and the United States and is currently Creative Director and will continue on and graduate with a Masters of Event Management with previous experience of studying a Bachelor of Communications majoring in Journalism and minoring in Public Relations.

Tahlia is very interested in pop culture media which includes music, movies, sports, video games and books. She has a keen interest in Formula 1 and has been for the past 2 years. She hopes one day she can attend the Melbourne, Monaco, Monza and Singapore grand prix's in the future
